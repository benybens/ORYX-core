# Bibliography

A living reference list. Add primary literature (papers, patents, vendor
datasheets) as each propulsion technology and subsystem is researched --
this seed list is limited to widely known, easily verifiable textbooks
covering the general disciplines involved, deliberately avoiding
fabricated or unverified citations.

## Propulsion & rocketry fundamentals

- Sutton, G. P., & Biblarz, O. *Rocket Propulsion Elements*. Wiley.
- Hill, P., & Peterson, C. *Mechanics and Thermodynamics of Propulsion*.
  Addison-Wesley.

## Aerodynamics & flight dynamics

- Anderson, J. D. *Fundamentals of Aerodynamics*. McGraw-Hill.
- Stevens, B. L., Lewis, F. L., & Johnson, E. N. *Aircraft Control and
  Simulation*. Wiley.

## Control allocation

- Johansen, T. A., & Fossen, T. I. (2013). "Control allocation -- A
  survey." *Automatica*, 49(5), 1087-1103.
